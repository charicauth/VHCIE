Announcement


Rejected papers from IEEE VR conference track can be considered for oral presentation to our workshop upon the following conditions:


  • Paper should fit the workshop topics
  • Paper will appear as abstract only, the content of the paper will not be part of the IEEE Digital library
  • Authors should send us the complete version of the rejected paper as well as the summary of the reviews so as to make a decision to accept this contribution to the workshop (the paper and the meta review will be kept strictly confidential between the VHCIE organizers)
  • Decision will be sent before February 1st

1

Submissions must be in PDF format, using the IEEE VR formatting guidelines.

2

Paper length:

  • - Research papers : 4-6 pages
  • - Technical notes : 2-4 pages
  • - Position papers : 2-4 pages
  • - Abstract only (not included in publications, but with oral presentation) : 1 page

3

Papers should be submitted by email to vhcie.vr@gmail.com.

Papers (not abstracts) will be included in the IEEE Xplore library and will have unique ISBN.
